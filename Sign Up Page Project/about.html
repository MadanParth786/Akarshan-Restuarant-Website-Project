About us



Akarshan Restaurant is a popular eatery in Nurpur, Himachal Pradesh. It is known for its delicious and traditional Indian cuisine.The restaurant specializes in North Indian dishes, though they also offer a variety of Chinese, South Indian and Continental dishes as well. The restaurant offers an extensive menu of both vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes. Akarshan Restaurant is a great option for those looking for a casual dining experience in a relaxed atmosphere. The restaurant also features a live music band most nights, which adds to the atmosphere. The restaurant is known for its friendly service and reasonable prices.The restaurant has a cozy and warm atmosphere, making it a great place to hang out with friends or family. The restaurant also offers a variety of takeaway services. Akarshan Restaurant is a great place to enjoy delicious food at an affordable price. The menu includes a variety of dishes such as: tandoori chicken, malai kofta, paneer tikka masala, veg kofta, paneer chilli, and many more. The restaurant also offers some delicious desserts like gulab jamun, rasmalai, and many more. The staff at Akarshan Restaurant is friendly and attentive, making sure that every customer has a great dining experience.


About Nurpur Town



Nurpur is a small town in the Kangra district of Himachal Pradesh, India. It is situated on the banks of the Beas River, near the border of Punjab. Nurpur was once the capital of a princely state of the same name, which was founded by the Pathania Rajputs in the 17th century. The town is famous for its fort and temple, and is home to a number of old monuments, temples, and other historical sites. The area is also known for its scenic beauty, with the Dhauladhar mountain range in the background. Nurpur is well connected by road and rail to nearby cities such as Pathankot and Amritsar, as well as other parts of Himachal Pradesh.Nurpur is an important pilgrimage site for Hindus and is home to several ancient temples, including the famous Bhawani Amba temple. It also has some stunning natural attractions, such as the Kiratpur waterfall, which is a popular spot for picnics. Nurpur is also known for its local handicrafts, such as wood carvings and hand-woven shawls. The town is also home to a number of renowned artisans and craftsmen who specialize in creating intricate and beautiful pieces of art.
